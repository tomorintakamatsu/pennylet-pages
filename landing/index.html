Questions before you try it?

Short answers for the things people usually want to know before adding another finance app.

Is PennyLet a full budgeting app?

Yes. It tracks spending, income, balance, categories, goals, subscriptions, and budget health, but the main habit is simpler: check what is safe to spend today.

Can I use PennyLet without linking a bank?

Yes. PennyLet is built around manual entry, receipt scanning, CSV import/export, and local budget setup instead of requiring a bank connection.

What does Pro add?

Pro expands AI analysis limits, adds forecasts, unlocks custom categories and visual charts, and removes the monthly receipt scan limit.

Does PennyLet work with multiple currencies?

Yes. You can enter supported foreign-currency purchases and keep the original amount visible alongside your base-currency tracking.
