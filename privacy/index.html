PennyLet Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

PennyLet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the PennyLet mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in via Base44, we receive your account identifier, email address, and name from the authentication provider. We do not collect or store your password.

Financial Data: The App allows you to log transactions, budgets, savings goals, income, and subscription details. This data is stored on Base44 servers and is associated with your account when you create one.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency and error logs to improve the App.

Device Permissions:

  • Camera: Used only for receipt scanning. Photos are processed to extract transaction details and are not stored permanently.
  • Microphone and Speech Recognition: Used only for voice input of transactions. Audio is processed for transcription and is not used for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide budget tracking, spending summaries, receipt scanning, goals, and subscription tracking.
  • To generate AI-powered spending insights when you use AI features.
  • To manage subscription status and usage limits.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To improve the App through aggregated or anonymized analytics.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on your device and synced to our secure cloud backend when you create an account. You can use the App in guest mode without creating an account; in that case, data stays only on your device.

4. AI Features

AI analysis features send financial summaries to our AI service for processing. We avoid sending personally identifiable information in these requests. AI features are used only to provide the App's finance insights.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may export your data, delete app data, or request account and data deletion by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at clearspendapp@gmail.com.
