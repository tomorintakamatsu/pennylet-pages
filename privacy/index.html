PennyLet Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

PennyLet is a local-first iPhone budgeting app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Information You Add to the App

PennyLet lets you enter and manage budgeting information, including:

  • Transactions, amounts, categories, dates, notes, and merchant names.
  • Income, budget settings, savings goals, and expense limits.
  • Subscription information you choose to track.
  • Receipt images or receipt details when you use receipt scanning.

PennyLet does not require an account or a bank connection.

2. Local Storage

Your core budgeting data is stored on your device. If you delete the app or reset app data, local data may be removed. You can export CSV data from the app if you want to keep a copy.

3. Optional AI and Receipt Features

AI features are optional. When you request an AI insight or receipt extraction, PennyLet may send the information needed for that request, such as spending summaries or receipt content, to an AI processing service so the app can return the result. Do not include sensitive personal information in notes or receipts if you do not want it processed for these optional features.

4. Purchases and Subscriptions

PennyLet Pro purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store. PennyLet uses Apple purchase information to unlock Pro features.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: Used when you choose to scan a receipt.
  • Notifications: Used only if you allow reminders or app notifications.

6. How We Use Information

  • To show budgets, transactions, goals, subscriptions, charts, and spending summaries.
  • To provide receipt scanning and optional AI insights when you request them.
  • To respond to support or privacy requests.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete local app data using the app's reset tools or by deleting the app from your device. For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us at f32pluspai@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

PennyLet is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

Contact us at f32pluspai@gmail.com.
