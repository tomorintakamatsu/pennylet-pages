Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

ClearSpend ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the ClearSpend mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in via Base44, we receive your account identifier, email address, and name from the authentication provider. We do not collect or store your password.

Financial Data: The App allows you to log transactions, budgets, savings goals, and income. This data is stored on Base44 servers and is associated with your account.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency and error logs to improve the App.

Device Permissions:

  • Camera: Used only for receipt scanning. Photos are processed to extract transaction details and are not stored permanently.
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition: Used only for voice input of transactions. Audio is processed on-device and is not recorded or stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (budget tracking, spending analysis)
  • To generate AI-powered spending insights based on your transaction data
  • To manage your subscription and usage limits
  • To communicate with you about your account or respond to support requests
  • To improve the App through aggregated, anonymized analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Base44's secure cloud infrastructure. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Base44: Provides backend data storage, authentication, and AI function invocation. See Base44's privacy policy for details on their data handling.
  • RevenueCat (if subscribed): Manages subscription purchases and verification. RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase receipts.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your financial data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete individual transactions, goals, or your entire account through the App. Account deletion permanently removes all associated data from Base44.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App.
